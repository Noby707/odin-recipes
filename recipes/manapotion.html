Mana Potion

Description:

A mana potion that gives 50 points of mana.

Ingredients:

  • Slime Core
  • mana gem
  • Moon stalk grass
  • purified water

Steps

  1. Grind moon stalk grass
  2. Grind mana gem
  3. Grind Slime core
  4. Fill culdorn with purified water and spin it
  5. Add all Ingredients to culdorn
  6. Add mana to grinded slime core to start reaction
  7. Store in enchanted glass and cover
